To get started quickly, follow these recommended steps and formats.

Preferred formats

  • MP4 (H.264) or MOV
  • High bitrate, no excessive compression
  • Separate audio files are helpful for higher quality

How to send large files

  • Use Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer or any cloud link
  • Organize clips into folders and label them clearly
  • Include a short text file with deadlines and special instructions

Checklist before upload

  • Confirm resolution and frame rate
  • Provide music or assets (if any) and licensing info
  • Write timestamps or notes for important moments
